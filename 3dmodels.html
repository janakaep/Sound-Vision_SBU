Small Conference Room Specifications

The small conference room is designed for 1-4 people, providing essential A/V capabilities for intimate meetings and discussions.

Equipment List:

  • One 43" television monitor on wall mount
  • CATV connection routed directly to display
  • Sound through display speakers
  • TV's IR remote for channel selection and volume control
  • Wall-mounted cable management system
  • Standard conference table for up to 4 people

Medium Conference Room Specifications

The medium conference room accommodates 5-10 people and features enhanced A/V capabilities for effective team collaboration and presentations.

Equipment List:

  • One 65" display on tilting wall mount
  • Airtame 2 wireless presentation system via MWAA Wi-Fi
  • CATV connection for television viewing
  • Pan/Tilt/Zoom web camera with USB connection
  • HDMI extension system from table to display
  • USB microphone/speaker for video conferencing
  • Sound bar mounted under display for audio amplification
  • TV's IR remote for room control
  • Conference table with integrated cable management

About the MWAA A/V Implementation Project

Sound and Vision Design is proud to present our comprehensive A/V implementation proposal for the Metropolitan Washington Airports Authority. This project encompasses the design, installation, and support of audio-visual systems across multiple MWAA facilities.

Project Overview

Our implementation plan includes:

  • Standardized conference room designs for small, medium, and large meeting spaces
  • Custom solutions for specialized areas like the Board Conference Room and Emergency Operations Centers
  • Integration with existing MWAA infrastructure and systems
  • Comprehensive training and documentation
  • Ongoing maintenance and support services

Service Locations

The implementation will cover three primary service locations:

  • MWAA Corporate Headquarters (CHQ) - 26 conference rooms including specialized areas
  • Reagan National Airport (DCA) - 8 conference rooms and specialized facilities
  • Dulles International Airport (IAD) - 16 conference rooms and specialized facilities

The interactive 3D models presented here were created to provide a visual representation of our proposed A/V implementations. They demonstrate the layout, equipment placement, and overall design of each room type according to the specifications outlined in the RFP.
