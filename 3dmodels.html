Small Conference Room Specifications

The small conference room is designed for 2-5 people, providing essential A/V capabilities for intimate meetings and discussions.

Equipment List:

  • Airtame 2 wireless presentation system
  • 43" 4K display with wall mount
  • Integrated audio through display speakers
  • Secure network connectivity
  • Simple user controls with remote interface

Medium Conference Room Specifications

The medium conference room accommodates 6-9 people and features enhanced A/V capabilities for effective team collaboration and presentations.

Equipment List:

  • Airtame 2 wireless presentation system with dual-band connectivity
  • 65" 4K display with tilting wall mount
  • Pan/Tilt/Zoom camera with USB connectivity
  • Sound bar with integrated microphone array
  • HDMI extension system for wired presentation options
  • Dedicated network access point with VLAN segmentation

Large Conference Room Specifications

The large conference room is designed for 10-24 people with comprehensive A/V solutions for board meetings, presentations, and video conferencing.

Equipment List:

  • Dual Airtame 2 devices for redundant wireless presentation
  • 109" diagonal projection screen with 4K short-throw projector
  • 75" interactive touch monitor for collaborative content
  • Ceiling-mounted microphone array with DSP processing
  • Integrated audio system with four recessed ceiling speakers
  • PTZ camera system for videoconferencing
  • Crestron control system with 7" touch panel interface
  • Enterprise-grade networking with dedicated VLAN and QoS settings

About the MWAA A/V Implementation Project

Sound and Vision Design is proud to present our comprehensive A/V implementation proposal for the Metropolitan Washington Airports Authority. This project encompasses the design, installation, and support of audio-visual systems across multiple MWAA facilities.

Project Overview

Our implementation plan includes:

  • Standardized conference room designs for small, medium, and large meeting spaces
  • Custom solutions for specialized areas like the Board Conference Room and Emergency Operations Centers
  • Integration with existing MWAA infrastructure and systems
  • Comprehensive training and documentation
  • Ongoing maintenance and support services

Service Locations

The implementation will cover three primary service locations:

  • MWAA Corporate Headquarters (CHQ) - 26 conference rooms including specialized areas
  • Reagan National Airport (DCA) - 8 conference rooms and specialized facilities
  • Dulles International Airport (IAD) - 16 conference rooms and specialized facilities

About These 3D Models

The interactive 3D models presented here were created to provide a visual representation of our proposed A/V implementations. They demonstrate the layout, equipment placement, and overall design of each room type according to the specifications outlined in the RFP.
